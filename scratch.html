My Photography Process

  1. Consultation
    • Discuss with the client their vision
    • Choose the best location
    • Set a date & time
  2. Photo Shoot
    • Bring all my camera equipment
    • Be creative with shots, angles, and lighting
    • Guide the client in poses and prompts
  3. Editing
    • Upload and cull through all the RAWs
    • Apply presets, color grade, and crop
    • Add finishing touches and export
  4. Delivery
    • Assemble & style the entire gallery
    • Send the client an email
    • Give client high-resolution downloads
